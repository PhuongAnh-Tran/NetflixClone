ANH IN MONTREAL

2025 Limited Series HD

A neuroscience grad turned full-stack developer, Anh blends logic with creativity—one project at a time.

Cast: Phuong Anh Tran (Anh)

Genres: Tech, Creativity, Web Development

This show is: Analytical, Bilingual, Resourceful

Episodes

1

The Spark

Anh builds her first prototype at UpGen Bootcamp. The excitement is real—but so is the pressure to make her parents proud. Neuroscience feels like the ‘right’ path... but is it?

2

The Science Years

She chooses Neuroscience at Université de Montréal, thinking med school is the goal. Labs, research, data—it’s all interesting, but something is missing… creativity.

3

Breaking the Code

After earning her degree, Anh makes a choice: follow expectations or follow creativity? She takes the leap and pursues a Computer Science BSc at Université de Montréal, where she learns to code—front-end, back-end, APIs. This time, problem-solving feels right.

4

Creative Code in Action

Anh applies her skills to real-world projects, creating AI-powered and interactive web apps that merge creativity with functionality.

5

Sid Lee Bound?

Anh is ready for the next chapter—with her passion for UX/UI and web development, she’s ready to bring her skills to Sid Lee and create something extraordinary.

Behind the Scenes
